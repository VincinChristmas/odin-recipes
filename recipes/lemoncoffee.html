Lemon Coffee

Hot Lemon Coffee

Let me tell you how to make lemon coffee. This kind of cofee is very relaxing and is very delicious.

Ingredients

  • coffee beans
  • a tablespoon of absolute vodka
  • a thin slice of lemon
  • a lighter to make fire

The Steps

  1. grind the beans and make a cup of coffee
  2. cut a thin slice of lemon and put it on top
  3. spread while pouring a tablespoon of vodka on top of the coffee mixture.
  4. light a small fire on top with a lighter and let it burn out by itself
  5. can add a little sugar for flavor. Drink and enjoy.
